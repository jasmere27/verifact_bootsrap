About Us

VeriFact: AI-Powered Fake News Detection

VeriFact is a cutting-edge platform that helps users identify and filter out fake news by leveraging advanced artificial intelligence techniques. Our system analyzes both text and image content to determine the credibility of online information in real time.

In a digital world where misinformation spreads rapidly, VeriFact empowers individuals to make informed decisions and promotes a healthier information ecosystem. Whether you're verifying an article or fact-checking an image, our tools are designed to provide accurate and transparent analysis.

Key Technologies Powering VeriFact

VeriFact is built on a powerful blend of modern technologies that work together to detect and flag fake news. Here's a breakdown of the core systems that make VeriFact reliable and intelligent.

Artificial Intelligence (AI)

Our AI models analyze the language structure, claims, and credibility of news articles to detect misinformation patterns.

Explore AI
VeriFact AI Agent

Our custom-trained VeriFact AI Agent uses advanced machine learning models to evaluate news content, detect misinformation patterns, and return reliable results in real time.

Learn More about AI Agent
Optical Character Recognition (OCR)

Using Tesseract OCR, VeriFact extracts text from news images to analyze their content just like typed text.

Learn more About OCR
Real-Time API Integration

VeriFact instantly sends data to a backend AI via REST APIs for rapid analysis, returning verdicts within seconds for both text and image-based news.
